Nutrition Tips

Eating a variety of foods, including plenty of vegetables, fruits, and whole grains, can help you maintain energy levels and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Fitness Advice

Engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily. Activities like walking, cycling, or swimming can improve cardiovascular health and mood.
